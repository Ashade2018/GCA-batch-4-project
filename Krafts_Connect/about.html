About Krafts_Connect


Krafts_Connect is a social site that brings to one stop a home for indigenous craftsmen. it is true that indigenous crafts are a viable source of creating wealth and if fully explored through industrialization can boost foreign reserve and create employment.

It aims to bring to view craftsmen and fellow crafts enthusiasts under various categories

Categories Like:
  • Crochet and Weaving: different crafts from cloth making to hats, carpet, rugs,chairs,bgs. basket, fans,mats and other items.
  • Leather Works: bags, footwear, belts, football,clothes and furniture
  • Blacksmithing and GoldSmithing: farming tools, litchen utensils, jewellery,beautiful stones, and trinkets.
  • Pottery: Pots,calabashes,bowls,mugs,pipes
  • Carving: wooden trays, stools,chest, jewellery boxes, candle stands, walking sticks, panelled doors and jewellery
  • Dyeing
  • Painitng
  • Calligraphy and graphic arts
  • Soap Making
  • SMES: other small medium enterprises not listed
