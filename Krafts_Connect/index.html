Krafts_Connect is a social niche that brings to one site various craftsmen and crafts enthusiasts.
It is your one-stop to meet indigenous craftsmen and have their location known.
It is believed crafts and arts are a means of communicating our emotions in a tangible form for the development of the society in other to better our lives.

There are various indigenous crafts in Nigeria and amongst the many, the following categories of crafts can be found at Krafts_Connect.
They are: 1. WEAVING:
This encompasses different crafts from cloth making to hats, carpet, rugs, chairs, bags, basket, fans, mats and other items.
Weaving can be done with different materials like wool, raffia, palm fronds, canes, twine and several others.

2. LEATHER WORKS: This encompasses all products that can be gotten from leather.
Leather can be processed to produce bags,footwear,wallets,belts,football,clothes and furniture.

3.BLACKSMITHING: craftsmen under this category are responsible for the production of tools like cutlasses to farmers.
We also have kitchen utensils, gates.
The goldsmiths are not left out as well for they make jewelleries with beautiful stones and trinkets.

OTHER CATEGORIES INLCLUDE:
4. Pottery
5. Carving
6. Dyeing
7. Painting
8. Calligraphy and graphic arts
9. Soap making
10. SMES- Small Medium Enterprises.
